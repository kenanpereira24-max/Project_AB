A Home for Orphan Kids – Service Since 2010

SNEHA JYOTHI ORPHAN CHILDREN'S HOME (R) was started on 19th March 2010 at Thagachaguppe Village and authorized by the Government of Karnataka. It is located in a remote area on the outskirts of Bengaluru city.

In this region, many orphaned children struggle without food, shelter, or family. They are forced to survive by working in homes, doing petty work, street begging, and other unsafe activities. At SNEHA JYOTHI, we aim to give these helpless children a safe and caring environment.

Our home provides good education, food, and shelter to help children live with dignity in society. This is the core purpose behind establishing our orphan children’s home as a trust.

Our Vision & Mission

For Education – Our Promise
To always remember that every child’s life is priceless and to put the needs of the children above our own.

To uphold the highest ethical standards in our work and to be honest and accountable in the reports about the children we help.

To recognize that none of our work is possible without the support of our donors, friends, and volunteers.

Feed for Hungry Child

We ensure that every child receives nutritious meals every day. No child should sleep with an empty stomach.

Home for the Homeless

We give abandoned and homeless children a protected home where they can live safely, grow happily, and build a brighter future.
